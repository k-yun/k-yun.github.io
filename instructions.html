First things first. Once you have a recipe, there are steps you will need to follow.

  1. Choose a recipe.

    2. There are so many to choose from. Don't get discouraged.

  2. Choose the right baking pan(s).

    3. Round. Square. Rectangle. You can't go wrong.

  3. Allow the ingredients to come to room temperature.

    4. An important step so all the ingredients mix well together.

  4. Prep the pan(s).

    5. You don't want to serve the cake out of the pan, do you?

  5. Preheat the oven.

    6. You want the oven waiting for you. You don't want to wait for the oven.

  6. Sift the dry ingredients together.

    7. No clumps!

  7. Cream the butter and sugar together.

    8. To make the cake light and fluffly

  8. Add the eggs...one at a time!!!

    9. Each egg takes time to incorporate into the butter/sugar mixture. Give each egg their own time.

  9. Alternately add the dry and wet ingredients.

    10. Dumping and stirring the batter will create a dense cake. If that's how you like it, knock yourself out. For a fluffier texture, add the dry ingredients slowly to prevent losing all that air incorporated from creaming the butter and sugar earlier.

  10. Pour batter into prepared pan(s).

    11. What are you waiting for?

  11. Check cake for doneness.

    12. There's a difference between a cake that's moist and a cake that's not done. Insert a skewer into the center of the cake and if it comes out clean, it's done.

  12. Cool the cake.

    13. This helps the cake set and not crumble apart. This can take anywhere from 10 minutes upto an hour.

  13. Assemble the cake.

    14. Stack the cake with a layer of frosting or jam (or both) in between.

  14. Frost!

    15. Would you eat a cone without ice cream?
